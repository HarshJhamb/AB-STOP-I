Our Story

Over 25 years of pioneering exhibition logistics, connecting businesses with global opportunities

We Are AB STOP

AB STOP India has been aiming high since it was founded in 2012 and today it stands as a worldwide and domestic logistics company that has grown to be one of the leading event logistics companies. The idea of S.T.O.P. (Sustainable Transport Organization of Professionals) originated back in 2001 and the journey started with providing services to travelers under STOP an alternative tourism. The services further diversified in 2012 to move both people and cargo. AB STOP India is now seen as one of the few India forwarders specializing in true customer service for its’ clients. With over 25 years of industry experience and expertise, our customers are at the heart of everything we do.

Mission & Vision

Defining our commitment to excellence in exhibition logistics

To be the most trusted exhibition logistics partner for businesses moving products across borders. We deliver time-critical cargo with zero delays, complete reliability, and hassle-free service—enabling companies to focus on their exhibitions while we handle the complexity of global logistics.

Our Vision

To create a seamless, globally-connected exhibition logistics ecosystem where every business—regardless of size—can confidently participate in international trade shows with complete peace of mind. We envision zero delays, zero stress, and maximum impact for every exhibition.

Our Journey

A timeline of milestones that shaped AB STOP India into an industry leader

1998
The journey began with a vision to simplify exhibition logistics across India and South Asia.
2012
AB STOP India officially registered as a dedicated exhibition logistics provider with operations across major Indian cities.
2015
Expanded international presence with partnerships in 30+ countries, establishing ourselves as a global player.
2018
Achieved major milestone of handling 500+ exhibitions annually with zero-delay record, earning industry recognition.
2021
Implemented advanced tracking systems and 24/7 support operations to enhance client experience globally.
2023
Reached 50+ countries with dedicated on-site handling partners, cementing our position as the preferred exhibition logistics leader.
