Gulfood is the world's largest and most influential food & beverage trade exhibition, held annually in Dubai, UAE. It's a premier platform for global food and beverage businesses.

Key Details:

  • Global Event: Thousands of exhibitors and buyers from 190+ countries
  • Participants: Food manufacturers, exporters, importers, distributors, hotels, retailers, and HoReCa brands
  • Products: Food & beverages, dairy, meat, grains, snacks, frozen food, health & organic products, and more
  • Venue: Dubai World Trade Centre
  • Duration: Usually held in February each year
