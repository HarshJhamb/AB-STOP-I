Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

Services & Shipping

What services do you offer?
We offer comprehensive exhibition logistics including air freight, sea freight, road transport, customs clearance, door-to-venue delivery, on-site supervision, and return logistics. All services are tailored for exhibition industry requirements.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary by route and method: Air freight typically 5-7 days, Sea freight 20-30 days, Road freight 3-5 days depending on destination. We work with strict exhibition deadlines and plan accordingly.
Do you handle customs clearance?
Yes! We specialize in customs clearance with expertise in ATA Carnet, temporary import procedures, and complex regulatory requirements across 130+ countries. Our documentation team ensures smooth border crossing.
Can you handle last-minute shipments?
Absolutely. We have emergency freight options and expedited services available. Contact our emergency support team immediately, and we'll arrange fastest possible transport to meet your exhibition deadline.
What industries do you specialize in?
We serve 6 major sectors: Food & Beverage, Electronics & Technology, Textile & Home Furnishing, Medical & Healthcare, Automotive & Machinery, and Printing & Packaging. Each has specialized handling procedures.

Logistics & Support

Do you provide on-site support?
Yes! We have official on-site handling agents at major exhibition venues globally. Our team handles last-mile delivery, stand placement, empty case storage, and provides support throughout the event.
How do I track my shipment?
Real-time tracking is available for all shipments. You'll receive tracking information via email with updates at each stage. You can also call our team for live updates 24/7 for time-critical shipments.
What if my cargo is damaged?
All our shipments are fully insured. In case of damage, report immediately with photos. We handle all insurance claims and coordinate replacement or repair within 48 hours to minimize impact on your exhibition.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 6-8 weeks before your exhibition for optimal planning. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines with expedited services. For emergency bookings, contact our emergency team immediately.
Do you offer storage facilities?
Yes! We have climate-controlled warehouses in major hubs for pre-exhibition storage, temperature-sensitive cargo, and empty case storage during events. Secure, organized, and professionally managed.
